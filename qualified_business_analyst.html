Qualified Business Analyst

2 years ago I received my BCS diploma in Business Analysis. I have garnered and honed the skills I have learnt and implement them in my day-to-day worklife. I am still gearing towards my International diploma.

Modelling Business Processes

Within the Modelling Business Processes Module I learned how to model business processes using Use Case Diagrams and Activity Diagrams. This module has helped me put names to processes I already picked up previously! I have modelled business processes in order to offshore processes with HSBC and I have model processes in my current role in order to make them more pragmatic and streamlined.

Requirements Engineering

Within the Requirements Engineering module I learned to elicit, document, and manage requirements. This is heavily used in my current role to elicit requirements for my project from key stakeholders who may or may not know they hold the knowledge. Meetings and interviews to elicit requirements, followed by documenting them, and managing them are a regular part of my day to day role
